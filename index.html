I am Boyuan An, an undergraduate student in Robotics Engineering (Honors Program) at ZheJiang University, expected to graduate in 2027. Currently, I am a research intern at the AI4CE Lab, New York University, working with Institute Associate Professor Chen Feng. Before that, I was fortunate to intern at ZJU FAST LAB, advised by Prof. Fei Gao.

My research interests include reinforcement learning, computer vision, and autonomous robotic systems.
